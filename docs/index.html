MineVRA: Exploring the Role of Generative AI in XR Content Development

Lorenzo Stacchio*1, Emanuele Balloni*2, Emanuele Frontoni*1, Marina Paolanti*1, Primo Zingaretti*2, Roberto Pierdicca*3

*1 University of Macerata, Department of Political Sciences, Communication and International Relations *2 Marche Polytechnic University, DII – Dipartimento di Ingegneria dell'Informazione *3 Marche Polytechnic University, DICEA – Dipartimento di Ingegneria Civile, Edile e Architettura

IEEE Conference on Virtual Reality and 3D User Interfaces, 2025
Paper Repository Vrai Lab

Abstract

MineVRA introduces a novel Human-In-The-Loop (HITL) XR framework integrating Generative AI for adaptive 3D content generation. The system enables real-time 3D object creation based on user prompts, leveraging text-to-image and image-to-3D pipelines. A comparative user study shows that AI-generated assets improve engagement and environmental coherence within immersive spaces. This research has strong implications for the future of AI-assisted XR content development.

MineVRA enables AI-driven 3D asset generation in XR environments.
